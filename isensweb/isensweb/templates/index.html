Facts

Senilis is a condition frequently occur on aged person that is similar to Arcus Juvenilis which also appears white or gray opaque ring in the eyes.

The white rings are caused by cholesterol deposits in or hyaline degeneration of the corneal stroma. In fact, on a statistics of an eye exam results made by Optum in behalf of United Healthcare group out of 72,534 persons the people with high cholesterol resulted to be 34,025, which is 46.9% of the total people who took the eye exam.

Constraints
